Since established in 2013, Digisailor has been offering world-class information technology.

Digisailor is an international IT consulting and Software development company with experience in multiple industries, including healthcare, retail, manufacturing, banking, and telecoms. Key domains of our expertise are IoT, big data, information security, mobile and web development, collaboration and knowledge management, and more.

  • 2013

    Once upon a time in 2013, Mr.Ilaiyaraja embarked on a journey to start his own company in the quaint coastal city of Tuticorin, located in the southern Indian state of Tamil Nadu. Armed with little more than a laptop and a big dream, He began laying the foundation for what would soon become Digisailor.

    Despite facing numerous challenges and obstacles, Ilaiyaraja persevered and gradually built up his team of like-minded individuals who shared his passion for technology and digital innovation. Together, they worked tirelessly to create cutting-edge solutions for their clients, leveraging the latest advancements in web design, software development, and digital marketing.

  • 2015

    In 2015, Digisailor hit a major milestone as the team moved into their first official office space. After years of working from home and coffee shops, the team was thrilled to have a dedicated space to call their own.

    Located in the heart of Tuticorin, the new office was a humble but comfortable space that provided the team with the resources and infrastructure they needed to take their work to the next level. With new equipment, more space to collaborate, and a professional environment to welcome clients, Digisailor was ready to take on the world.

  • 2017

    In just four short years, Digisailor had grown from a one-person operation to a thriving business with big ambitions. In 2017, the company reached another major milestone as they moved into a larger office and welcomed their 12th team member. With more space and talent, the Digisailor team was ready to take on even bigger and more complex projects.

    But that wasn't the only big news that year. In 2017, Digisailor also landed its first international clients, a major milestone for a company that had started out serving mainly local businesses. This new business not only allowed the team to expand their horizons and gain exposure to new industries and markets, but it also provided validation for the team's hard work and dedication to providing top-notch digital solutions.

  • 2019

    In 2019, Digisailor continued its meteoric rise, surpassing even its own ambitious goals. That year, the team grew to over 40 members, a testament to the company's dedication to hiring and retaining the best talent in the industry. To accommodate their growing team, Digisailor moved into a much larger office, complete with state-of-the-art technology, an open-concept design, and all the amenities the team could ask for.

    But that wasn't the only big news. In a major step towards expanding its global reach, Digisailor opened its first international office in the bustling city-state of Singapore. This marked an exciting new chapter for the company, as it began to establish a foothold in new markets and build relationships with international clients.


    And the good news didn't stop there. 2019 also saw Digisailor hit another major milestone, as they signed on their 500th client. This was a testament to the team's hard work, dedication, and innovative solutions, as well as a testament to the power of word-of-mouth marketing and referrals from satisfied customers.

  • 2021

    In just eight years, Digisailor had achieved incredible success and become a true global player in the world of digital innovation. In 2021, the company hit a new milestone as it welcomed its 80th team member and expanded its footprint with new offices in Malaysia, Germany, the USA, and Vietnam. These new locations allowed Digisailor to build even closer relationships with international clients and continue to deliver world-class digital solutions.

    But that wasn't the only news in 2021. Digisailor also formed key partnerships with major IT giants like Microsoft, Google, IBM, SAP, Amazon, and Flipkart. These partnerships enabled Digisailor to tap into the latest technologies and innovations, stay ahead of the curve, and continue to deliver cutting-edge solutions to its clients.

    And the good news kept coming. In 2021, Digisailor achieved ISO 9001:2015 and ISO 27001:2013 certifications, further validating the company's commitment to quality and security. These certifications were a testament to the team's hard work, dedication, and rigorous processes, and provided further assurance to clients that Digisailor was a reliable and trustworthy partner for all their digital needs.

  • 2023

    In 2023, Digisailor had reached new heights, becoming a leading player in the global digital innovation landscape. The company had expanded its team to over 120 members, with offices across India and new locations in Australia and Thailand. But Digisailor's commitment to excellence extended beyond its work and into its values and culture.

    The company had opened new offices in Tirunelveli, Chennai, and Tindivanam, expanding its reach and deepening its commitment to the communities it served. Digisailor continued to promote diversity and inclusivity in its hiring practices and workplace culture, ensuring that every team member felt valued, respected, and heard. It had established programs and initiatives to support employee mental health and wellbeing, fostering a positive, supportive work environment that nurtured creativity, innovation, and collaboration. Digisailor had become a carbon-neutral company, implementing sustainable practices throughout its operations to reduce its carbon footprint and protect the environment. This commitment to sustainability was just one example of Digisailor's broader dedication to social responsibility and equality.

    As the company continued to grow and innovate, it remained dedicated to upholding these values and setting new standards for digital innovation and responsible business practices. Digisailor's commitment to excellence, sustainability, and social responsibility had earned the company a reputation as a trustworthy and reliable partner for clients and a great place to work for team members.
